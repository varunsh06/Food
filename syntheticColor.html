Synthetic Food Colors

Food Colours - Also known as artificial food colors are chemically synthesized colors. We manufacture Food Colours as per International food color quality standards. Our food colors are extensively used in adding visual purpose to diverse food products and other utility products.

Food Colours or color additive is any dye, pigment or substance that imparts color when it is added to food or drink. We are supplying and exporting Food Colours in many forms consisting of liquids, Powder, Granular form. Our Food Colours are water soluble which are of high purity and has an application in Foodstuff Industries in accordance with various regulations.
